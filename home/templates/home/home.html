Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) approved Psych Institute of Management and Technology is the largest private polytechnic. Skilled human resources cannot be developed unless quality education is coordinated with the rapid spread of technology. And technical education is very important to build skilled and experienced manpower. With the evolution of time and the development of technology, the demand for technical education (Technical Education) is increasing worldwide. Psych Institute of Management and Technology strives to fulfill that need. Psych Institute of Management and Technology started its journey with only 1 technology in the month of June 2002 with sincere efforts and strong morale. In the first year of establishment, students were able to get admission in all the seats of the sanctioned number of seats. Facing the initial challenges, this private 4 polytechnic. (Technical zEducation) got permission to admit more students in more seats by introducing more new technologies from the academic year 2003-2004. At present, the program of admission of students in 1000 seats in 13 technologies is being conducted for the academic year 2021-2022.
